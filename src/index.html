NPO Saturn AL-41F1

The NPO Saturn AL-41F1 engine, an advanced turbofan powerplant. Originally developed for the previous-generation Su-35 fighter, this engine has proven its reliability with the advanced design incorporates advanced technologies and materials. The AL-41F1 engine has been recognized for its ability to provide the Su-35 with impressive maneuverability, high altitude performance, and a substantial speed envelope.

Cockpit

The cockpit of the Sukhoi Su-57 is equipped with advanced avionics, including large multi-function displays, a head-up display, and an integrated helmet-mounted display, providing the pilot with comprehensive situational awareness and intuitive control. The cockpit's ergonomic design, coupled with advanced communication and data link systems, enhances pilot comfort, operational efficiency, and effective coordination with other friendly forces.

Stealth and other features

Stealth design - reduce its radar cross-section and infrared signature. Air frame shape - carefully designed angles, contours, and shaping to deflect radar waves away from the source. Radar Absorbent Coatings - absorb and dissipate radar waves. Electronic Warfare Systems - advanced system that can detect and jam enemy radar signals.
